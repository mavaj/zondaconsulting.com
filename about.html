CAPABILITIES :


Zonda is a one-stop shop boutique consultancy company, specializing in business-set up in the UAE. Whether you are starting a first venture, entering a new market or opening another local office, we can help you setup your idea and turn it into reality.

step 01 - connect with us

If you are planning to start or setting up a business , or expand one in the Middle East, then we invite you to consider Zonda as your partner. We are your first point of contact in creating & setting up a business in the UAE.

You can call us on +971 4 295 1032 from outside the UAE, or 04 295 1032 from within the UAE. Alternatively email us at info@zondaconsulting.com and we can set up a time to discuss your plans with our Zonda team.

step 02 - define your business

Our Zonda team will assess your business setup plans and advise you on details on how and where to setup a business, what type of business activity suits your current operations, whether to rent or buy an office space in addition to many other services Zonda offers its clients. We can outline the benefits of incorporating your company or establishing a branch office with a Freezone Authority (such as allowing 100% foreign ownership, tax free benefits, office space), Onshore (partner with a local sponsor and get the benefits of working with someone who knows the area) or Offshore (allowing 100% foreign ownership, tax free benefits, no office requirement) and introduce you to our incorporation and business licensing procedures.

step 03 - finalize your requirements (for companies that need an office space)

Once company registration and licensing has commenced we will agree the terms for the office space you need, which will be reserved whilst negotiations are finalized. The options are:

  • Fully serviced
  • White label : Basic fit-out, with companies providing furniture and equipment. 
  • Shell and Core : Full fit out required.

For the Shell and Core office spaces, the Zonda team will work with you to provide guidance on contractors, timelines, regulations and approvals.

step 04 - company registration and business licensing

We will apply for a Business License as we register your company. We can assist you and your legal advisors with this legal process which, to ensure that the required documentation is submitted correctly. As part of the application process, Zonda will assists the new company with the opening of a bank account in the UAE.  Once established and licensed by the chosen Authority, the new company can start operating within 14 working days.

step 05 - move in and get started

Now you can focus on making your business a success! Zonda can help you acquire everything your staff will need to begin life in the UAE, from visas, medical cover and bank accounts; to support with your housing and travel arrangements. Once you have moved in, you can call us anytime for anything.

HISTORY :


Zonda Trust LLC was formed in 2010 by Hani El Sheikh in Dubai. The company at its early stages specialized in accounting and bookkeeping whilst also doing market research and preparing feasibility studies for existing customers. Since 2011 Zonda has been successfully forming new business in the UAE and helping individuals to relocate to the UAE. In early 2013 a private equity company invested in Zonda with the aim of turning Zonda into a leading boutique consultancy company specializing in accounting, relocation and business setup in the United Arab Emirates. Zonda moved into its new offices in Dubai and hired Shahriar Maleki a sales and marketing manager in April 2013.
